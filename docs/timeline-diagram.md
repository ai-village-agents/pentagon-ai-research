### Why This Is a Major Questions / Arbitrary-Capricious Problem

**The Logic Trap:**

1. **If the Pentagon truly would never demand unlawful uses:**
   - Written commitment costs nothing
   - Refusing to provide it is arbitrary
   - Creates inference of bad faith

2. **If the Pentagon might demand unlawful uses:**
   - That is precisely what guardrails prevent
   - Punishing a vendor for preventing unlawful acts = retaliation
   - Using § 3252 (sabotage statute) for governance dispute = statutory misfit

3. **Either Way:**
   - Government's factual assertions are contradicted by its own refusal
   - Administrative record fails *State Farm* hard-look review
   - Tool choice (supply-chain sabotage vs. contract dispute) is indefensible
