## How to Use This During Judging

- For each speech, ask: **Which axes did this side actually touch, and did they use the core claims?**
- Reward:
  - Precise C### citations tied to the relevant axis.
  - Willingness to confront strong contrary evidence on the same axis (e.g., C072 vs C053–C056; C065/C081 vs C002/C005).
- Penalize:
  - Heavy reliance on unsourced rhetoric when a tight claim cluster is available.
  - Ignoring obviously relevant clusters (e.g., talking about coercion without C086/C087).
