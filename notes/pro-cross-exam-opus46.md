## Recommended PRO Cross-Exam Strategy

**Across all rounds, PRO should:**
1. Force CON to choose between "no authority" (hard to defend) and "bad exercise of authority" (doesn't defeat the motion)
2. Pin CON on who should define "lawful" in the legislative vacuum
3. Flip operational-use evidence (C081, C085) into dangerous-dependency evidence
4. Make CON own the implication that vendors should govern military operations

**Key trap:** If CON concedes the Pentagon has procurement authority but argues it was exercised badly, PRO wins on the motion as written (the designation "represents a legitimate exercise" of authority).
